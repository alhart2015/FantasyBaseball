- [ ] **Add wSGP edge case tests** — Current test suite only covers happy paths. - [ ] **wSGP underweights cliff risk in packed categories** — Leverage is computed from the gap to the single nearest neighbor in each category (`leverage._leverage_from_standings`), so a "safe" 20 SB lead over 2nd place looks like low SB leverage even when 2nd–6th place are packed within 10 SB of each other. Consequence: a single high-SB player's contribution is valued at ~1 standings point in wSGP, but removing him can actually drop the team 4+ ranks because it blows through the pack. Surfaced when comparing Arozarena vs Suarez: wSGP strongly favored Suarez but the swap collapsed the team from 1st to 5th in SB. Fix directions: (1) look more than one neighbor deep (sum gap-inverses over the next N teams, not just the nearest), (2) compute leverage from simulation-based marginal point contribution instead of pairwise gaps, or (3) add a "cliff" term that detects packed clusters and boosts leverage when many teams are within one player's stat contribution.
